About Charlie Brown

Charlie Brown is the child hero of the newspaper comic strip Peanuts. He is meek, kind, innocent and gentle hearted with many anxieties.

  1. He wears a yellow shirt
  2. He is insecure
  3. He has a dog named snoopy
